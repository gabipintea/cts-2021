Title:
`Design, Implementation and Practical Research on an open-source IoT Home Automation system`

Problem:
The system can manage permission for specific entity usage, by different users, or custom cases. There
should be a dynamic method for changing the behavior of a specific entity and hide it or making it unusable,
at that specific moment in the process.

Advantages:
- Dynamically change a entity visibility/usage permission, during execution

Disadvantages:
- None
